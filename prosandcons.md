# Pros and cons of subscription-based applications 

 

 

Pros: 

Easier accessibility: Customers can access applications with ease with a small financial commitment. 

Improvements: Companies tend to improve their subscription-based applications so that it makes using the application easier for customers. 

Support: Companies tend to continue to support their applications with updates and improvements and also add new features to their app. 

Predictable cost for providers: It allows companies to make service improvements to make a better product. 

 

Cons: 

Rising prices: Due to inflation, most companies are forced to raise their prices for subscriptions, which makes it difficult for customers to afford the application. 

Cancellations: One major problem is the complicated cancellation techniques that companies make to make cancellations difficult. 

Fatique: Some people might experience subscription fatique from too many subscription based apps.
